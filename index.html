SLA and Timeline Knowledge Base

Standard Process Timelines

🎨 Design Submission Timeline
Design submission typically takes 7 to 8 working days. This may be earlier or later depending on workload. After submission: Minor feedback takes 1 to 2 working days. Major feedback may increase the timeline significantly.
🌐 Beta Site Creation
4 to 5 working days are required to complete the beta site after design approval.
✍️ Content Addition
Content addition usually takes 4 to 5 working days for 15 to 20 pages. Timeline may increase or decrease based on number of pages, team availability, and current workload.
🔍 Pre-QA Internal Verification Phase
Before sending to QA Specialist: The scorecard must be completed by the Indian PM who must verify the website properly before filling it out. The scorecard must also be verified by OS. This step must be completed 1 to 2 days before QA submission. This ensures no major issues are missed and the QA process runs smoothly.
🚀 Onboarding Pre-Release Tasks
Onboarding pre-release tasks require 1 working day to complete.
📊 Full Timeline Summary
Design Layout: 7-8 days → Design Feedback: 1-2 days (minor), varies (major) → Beta Site: 4-5 days → Content Addition: 4-5 days for 15-20 pages → Pre-QA Internal Check: 1-2 days before QA → Pre-Release: 1 day
